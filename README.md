# 💧 Water Dash

Water Dash is a browser-based educational game inspired by charity: water. Players control a jerrycan to collect clean water, avoid pollution, and help deliver safe water to communities.

## 🎮 How to Play

- Move the jerrycan using your mouse or touch controls.
- 💧 Collect water to increase your score.
- ☠️ Avoid pollution to keep water safe.
- 💊 Use purification items to reduce pollution.
- 🗑️ Avoid trash because it reduces your progress.

## ✨ Features

- Three difficulty levels:
  - Easy
  - Medium
  - Hard
- Three progressive levels
- Score and water collection tracking
- Pollution system
- Timer-based challenges
- Animated feedback effects
- Responsive design for desktop and mobile

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

- HTML5
- CSS3
- JavaScript
